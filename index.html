ABOUT

If bricks make a building then stories make a civilization. Like how the first man came into being, or how was gravity discovered. Stories bind a society irrespective of our caste, colour or tribe. They intrigue us, scare us, and make us laugh or inspire us. We all love stories. We at Let It Out believe Everyone has a story to tell. You have yours, and if you pay close attention to your story, you will realize that your stories are collections of your experiences. Let It Out is a storytelling platform that gives all stories their due importance. We host storytelling events where a small group of people share their stories ; any story about their success, failures, struggles or lessons; stories that helped them create their identities. At Let It Out, sharing a story isn’t mandatory. For us the comfort of the attendees is of utmost importance. We strive to provide a safe space where you feel completely at ease. Be it narrating your story or just listening. Through the stories, we find that we share the same fundamental experiences as others, and yet our experiences differ from each other because everyone has a different perspective. When you hear a story, you step out of your reality and place yourself in someone else’s shoes, and you get to experience their perspective and engage in their emotions, actions, and decisions. Simply put, stories help you learn to empathize with other people as you come to understand their point of view. So come and attend a Let It Out event, we assure you that you won’t leave without meeting like minded people and an experience worth cherishing..
